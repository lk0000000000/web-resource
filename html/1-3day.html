1-3 Day Trip

Packing for a one to three day trip is nice and simple: everything that you might possibly need will fit into a humble backpack.
A one day trip does not require much: your toiletries and clothes don't need to leave your home, since you will be returning the same day. Oftentimes, you won't even be needing a charger (though it's better to be safe than sorry), or, depending on your method of transport, a backpack at all!
Here's a tentative list:

  • Wallet, keys, phone
  • Headphones
  • Charger
  • Weather-appropriate clothing
You can choose to bring some toiletries like lip balm, perfume spray, and makeup, but it really isn't necessary if you're traveling light.
For a two to three days stay, you will need to bring a change of clothes. Depending on your scruples on personal hygiene, it can be one, two, or three shirts. As a general rule, a shirt can be worn twice without issue, so a two to three day packing list can look something like this:
  • 1 or 2 pairs of socks
  • 1 or 2 pairs of underwear
  • 1 shirt
  • Toiletry bag containing a toothbrush, toothpaste, floss, deodorant, bar soap
  • Wallet, keys, phone
  • Headphones
  • Charger
Everything else is already on your person. This list can further be modified to include any makeup or formal wear you might be needing.
