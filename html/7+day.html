1 Week+ Trip

A trip lasting longer than a week ideally implies you will have access to laundry facilities at some point during your stay. Seeing as this category can encompass stays lasting over a month, a laundry machine is non-negotiable, whether it be in-house or at a laundromat.
A trip lasting a week or more seems daunting to pack for on paper, but remember: each laundry cycle basically resets the amount of days you will really be packing for. Knowing this, your packing can be broken into the same manageable chunks of 5 days maximum (and can all fit into a backpack!). Accounting for variety, your packing list wouldn’t be much longer:

  • 4 pairs of socks
  • 4 pairs of underwear
  • 4 shirts
  • 2 pair of pants or skirt
  • 1 pair of shoes
  • Toiletry bag containing a toothbrush, toothpaste, floss, deodorant, bar soap
  • Wallet, keys, phone
  • Headphones
  • Charger
As always, this list omits the clothing that you will be wearing to your destination, so in total, you would have one more copy of some clothing items. For variety, I have added an extra shirt for your cycle, but realistically you could even add a fifth shirt for a total of six options to choose from. Your bottoms do not need to be as varied; with a total of three bottoms to choose from, you should have enough style permutations to last you a long while. You may have noticed I had added an extra pair of shoes. While I think for most situations you would be fine with just one pair, for an extended stay, you will need an extra pair for backup (for weather or variety reasons). Anything extra like formal wear and other trip-specific apparel can still comfortably fit into a larger backpack. Consider also bringing a shopping tote for groceries if you don’t want to unpack your entire life to reuse your backpack.
